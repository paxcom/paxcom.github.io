Complete Omni Channel Solution

Our complete Omni channel solution will help you from a to z of your commerce, it will start right from digitizing your product in PIM cloud, coming up with your eStore and terminals for different merchants.

Kinator will be integrated with your store to fetch orders and then check business rules like inventory status, location, ratings etc and pass on the order to appropriate channel for fulfilment.

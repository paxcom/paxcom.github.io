Inventory Tracking

Do not let your sales suffer due to stock-out.

One of the key challenges for the retailers is to keep products inventory updated that customers want and need in stock. Customers also lose their interest in the seller when the desired product or most demanded product on the marketplaces by the customer is out of stock.

Many marketplaces also inactive the product which is out of stock. Inactive products are not visible in search results, to have visibility brand need to keep updated inventory on multiple marketplaces.

What we solve for you?
  • Inventory alerts - Alerts to refill the out of stock products on multiple channels to avoid lost sales.
  • Geographical Inventory Tracking - Keep a track of inventory for the location based on mapping with stores.

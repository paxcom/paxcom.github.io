Listen to what public data is saying

This might be the most interesting feature for Digital Commerce! A lot of public data, for your products and for your competition, is available on the internet. We help you make a sense of all of this data.

What we solve for you?

  • Track competition reviews to find what customers want
  • Let individual brand managers see reviews for their own products

  • Maintain price hygiene by enforcing MAP (Minimum Advertised Price) policy
  • Identify which seller is selling a particular product at what price, what is the buy box price or minimum buying price.
